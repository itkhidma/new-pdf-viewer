Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

TL;DR: PDF Viewer does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PDF Viewer. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

PDF Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Location data (GPS coordinates, IP address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Contact lists or calendar data
  • The content of your PDF files
  • Browsing history or search queries outside the app

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Recent Files List: File paths and names of PDFs you've opened (last 10 files)
  • Bookmarks: Last viewed page number for each PDF
  • App Preferences: User settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you:

  • Clear the app data from Android settings
  • Uninstall the application
  • Manually clear the recent files list in the app

4. Permissions Required

PDF Viewer requires the following Android permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To read and display PDF files from your device. This permission is only used when you explicitly open a PDF file.
  • INTERNET: Reserved for future feature to open online PDFs. Currently not actively used. No data is transmitted over the internet.

5. Third-Party Libraries

PDF Viewer uses the following third-party libraries:

  • Syncfusion PDF Viewer: For rendering PDF files. Processes data locally only.
  • Flutter Framework: Open-source UI toolkit by Google
  • Path Provider & Shared Preferences: For local file and data management

None of these libraries collect or transmit any personal data.

6. No Advertisements or Tracking

PDF Viewer is completely free and does not contain:

  • Advertisements
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user monitoring

7. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches related to our servers. All data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

8. Children's Privacy

PDF Viewer is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

You have complete control over the local data by managing the app through Android settings.
